Recipe for Pancake

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar if necessary.
  • Dry Ingredients as strawberry, blueberry as wish.

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients,flour, sugar and mix.
  3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook on the other side.
